Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about dining, beach experiences, and safaris with Kibo Restaurant in Watamu.

What type of food does Kibo Restaurant serve?

We specialize in fresh seafood, Italian favorites, Swahili-inspired dishes, and vegetarian options — all prepared with a homely touch and the finest local ingredients.

Do you have vegetarian or gluten-free options?

Yes, our menu includes a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free dishes. Just let our team know your dietary needs and we’ll be happy to recommend the best choices.

Can we dine right by the beach?

Absolutely! Our beachfront setting lets you enjoy your meal with ocean views, cool breezes, and a relaxed coastal atmosphere.

Do you host private events and celebrations?

Yes, we host weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, and private parties. We can tailor the menu, décor, and setting to make your day unforgettable.

Do you offer local tours or safaris?

Through Kibo Safari Kenya, we arrange trips to Watamu Marine Park, Gede Ruins, and Kenya’s top safari destinations like Amboseli and Masai Mara.

How can I book a table or safari with you?

You can call us at +254 715408 584, message us on WhatsApp, or use the contact form on our website. For safaris, we recommend booking in advance.
